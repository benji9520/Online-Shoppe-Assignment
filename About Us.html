Founded in 2021, Smiles intends to promote fantastic local artists while simultaneously raising funds for charitable initiatives. Each Month, a new fundraising intiiative is announced. Smiles then connects with local artists to se if they are up for the challenge of creating art to sell taking into account the monthly theme. Usually it is all smile related.

Everyone has been down and out at some point in their life. Smiles intends to lessen the load.
